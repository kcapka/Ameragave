THE AGAVE HEART

While our ultimate goal is to cultivate a fully estate-grown agave farm in America, today we source our 100% Blue Weber Agave from one of the industry's top-tier NOMs, nestled in the heartland of agave: San Julian, Jalisco.

Our process begins with the agave's 'pi√±a' or heart. These are slowly steam-roasted in traditional, thick stone-walled brick ovens. This gentle process, which spans between 36 to 54 hours, unlocks the agave's natural sugars and imparts a depth of flavor that's truly unique.

We believe in taking our time, especially when it comes to fermentation. Our fermentation process is intentionally slow, averaging between 48 to 60 hours. While many mass-produced tequilas undergo a swift 10-12 hour fermentation, our elongated process allows for a fuller-bodied agave flavor.

Our spirit is then double distilled, extracting only the purest distillate. The result is an ultra-premium spirit of unparalleled quality and flavor, but we're not done yet.

NO ADDITIVES

Many tequilas and other agave spirits contain additives, even "100% agave" tequilas, according to Mexican law, are allowed to contain up to 1% of additives by volume. Many brands, in the pursuit of specific flavors or appearances, resort to the addition of additives. Some use glycerin to achieve a smoother texture, while others might incorporate caramel color to mimic the rich hue of aged spirits. Sugar syrups can be added to sweeten the profile, oak extracts to imitate the nuances of barrel aging, and citric acid to adjust acidity.

But here at Ameragave, we tread a different path.

Our commitment is to the purity and integrity of the agave's natural flavors. That's why you won't find any additives in our products. No colorings, no added sugars, and no flavor enhancers. Just the pure, nuanced taste of 100% blue weber agave, captured in its most genuine form.

Why no additives? It's simple, we don't need them. Creating a high-quality spirit without additives requires a higher level of craftsmanship. It entails meticulous control over the every step of the process. With Amerigave™ agave spirits, you're experiencing the true essence of the agave, unadulterated and unmatched. Purely Ameragave™.

AGING, THE AMERAGAVE™ WAY

We asked ourselves: why wait years for tequila to rest in barrels when American ingenuity could pave a new path? Our unique Gold maturation process not only surpasses the flavor of ultra-premium tequilas but also only uses one-fifth of the amount of oak compared to traditional barrel aging.

This meticulous approach ensures a taste that stands out from the rest while maintaining the essence of Blue Weber Agave. Our Gold variant is crafted for depth and complexity, comparable to the finest traditionally aged spirits.

NEW AMERICAN OAK

While many tequilas are aged in reused barrels that have already imparted their flavors into previous spirits, Ameragave takes a different approach. We believe in the character and depth that only new American oak can bring to our Gold variant. Why settle for a barrel's second act when you can capture the magic of its premiere?

By choosing to age our Gold Agave Spirit with new American oak, we tap into the rich tannins, caramel notes, and complex flavors that unused wood provides. The result is an exceptionally rich, layered experience that complements the natural profiles of our Blue Weber Agave. It's a choice that not only reflects our commitment to unparalleled quality but also sets us distinctly apart in the landscape of agave spirits. Why sip tequila when you can relish in a refined, groundbreaking creation that elevates the agave spirit experience?

SUSTAINABILITY AT HEART

This innovative method not only yields an incredible tasting, beating ultra-premium tequilas in blind taste tests but also contributes to a more sustainable process. It uses just one-fifth of the oak used in traditional barrel aging, with no angel's share lost due to evaporation.

Traditional aging demands a high amount of oak, leading to deforestation and environmental strain. Our process dramatically reduces the need for wood, creating a more eco-friendly spirit.

No Angel's Share: Evaporation during the aging process isn't just a poetic term; it's a significant loss. Our technology drastically reduces this, ensuring more spirit is saved for sipping.

The term 'angel's share' may sound poetic, but it signifies a real and often substantial loss due to evaporation during aging. Our technology eliminates this, which is not only better for our planet, but also for your palate, ensuring more spirit is saved for sipping.
